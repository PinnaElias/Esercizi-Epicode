Focaccia with caramelized Onions, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients:

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350 g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon of olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces of Brie, cut into cubes
  • 1 1/2 cups of fresh or frozen cranberries
